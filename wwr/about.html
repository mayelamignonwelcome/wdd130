About Rafting Company Rapids

Our Story

Founded with a passion for adventure and nature, Rafting Company Rapids has grown from a small local outfitter into a trusted guide for whitewater experiences. Our mission is to provide safe, fun, and memorable trips for all ages.

History

Rafting Company Rapids was founded in 2010 by two river enthusiasts who wanted to share their love of whitewater with the community. Starting with just a single raft on the local river, the company quickly grew thanks to word of mouth and a commitment to safety and fun. By 2015, we expanded to offer multiple guided trips and training programs, welcoming adventurers from all over the world.
